Our Story

3 Friends, 1 Summer, Infinite Inspiration!


Three friends embarked on a summer adventure, traveling around the Mediterranean. As they explored the vibrant cities and charming towns, they discovered a common love for delicious and unique ice cream flavors. However, when the Covid-19 pandemic hit, their plans for future travels were put on hold. Instead, they found themselves stuck at home, trying to find a way to make the most of the situation.


That's when they decided to turn their passion for ice cream into a business. They began experimenting with different flavors and recipes, and soon, they had a collection of mouth-watering ice creams that were unique and delicious. They launched their business online, and to their surprise, it took off quickly. Now, with a gorgeous cafe allowing them to meet people in person, and a new van taking them back on the road, Scoop It Up! has grown from strength to strength!

And it's all thanks to you....


Without our amazing customers, we'd still just be hanging out in the kitchen, wondering what to do with all the ice cream we'd made!
